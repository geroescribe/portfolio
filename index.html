About Me

At 28, I decided to leave my own business and start developing as a front-end developer. Most of my adulthood was spent surrounded by books, and as a writer, I wanted to give myself the opportunity to be creative by adding another way.

Having made a career change requires constant willpower. The challenges that are faced are new and often it is not even known what the initial problem is. However, perseverance is one of the defining characteristics of my career.

Now, through programming, I also tell stories. The possibilities are endless and with my background as an entrepreneur, I have the discipline and that outside-the-box critical thinking to achieve the seemingly impossible.

When I grew up I learned that it is never too late to start over.
